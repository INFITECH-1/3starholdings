Company Description

3 Star Holdings (Pvt) Ltd is a limited liability company registered under the Company Act No. 07 of 2007 in Sri Lanka.
We have a good experience in a broad field covering from small buildings to massive projects such as water supply projects, ports, high rise buildings etc.
We are specialized for water supply projects.

About Us

Managing Director RMKJ Bandara, who is also a qualified Labour Administrator under the Departments of Labour, formed a Labour Supply Company (JB Constructions, Man Power Supply Co. & 3 Star Labour & Mechinery Supply Co. (Pvt) Ltd) in 1994 and provided skill/unskill workers and Office Staff/Managers and Engineering Staff to various local and foreign companies for their construction projects in various parts of Sri Lanka. Thereafter this company was re-organised as 3 Star Holdings (Pvt) Ltd.
Subsequently this company was upgraded to a limited liability company as to expand its services.
