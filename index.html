Company Description

3 Star Holdings (Pvt) Ltd is a leading provider of comprehensive construction solutions in Sri Lanka. We specialize in supplying skilled and unskilled manpower, along with a wide range of machinery and equipment for construction projects of all scales.

Our History

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the construction industry in Sri Lanka, our company has evolved from JB Constructions into 3 Star Holdings, bringing decades of experience and expertise to every project we undertake.
